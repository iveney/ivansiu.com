Classical Music

So…

I will try to document my notes of listening to Classical music here. My initial goal is to get familiar with famous pieces and composers, such as Beethoven, Chopin, J.S. Bach and Tchaikovsky.

Logs

  • 4/20/2014. Start to listen The Six suites for unaccompanied cello by Johann Sebastian Bach. There are several famous versions. I start by listening to Yo Yo Ma’s version.

Glossary

  • opus: piece of music numbered by composers. For instance, Beethoven’s “Moonlight Sonata” is labeled “Op. 27, No. 2” (opus 27, number 2), meaning it is the companion piece to another sonata, Op. 27 No. 1.
  • BWV: The Bach-Werke-Verzeichnis (Bach Works Catalogue) is the numbering system identifying compositions by Johann Sebastian Bach.
